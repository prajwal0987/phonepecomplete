PhonePe Payment Gateway – KYC Process

PhonePe Payment Gateway enables merchants to accept digital payments securely and efficiently. As per regulatory requirements, completing KYC is mandatory to continue uninterrupted payment services.

Why is KYC important?
KYC helps verify merchant identity, ensures compliance with RBI guidelines, and prevents fraudulent transactions.

Benefits of completing KYC:

  • Seamless payment acceptance
  • Faster settlements
  • Regulatory compliance
